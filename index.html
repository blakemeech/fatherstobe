Tournament Info

  • Modified Pinehurst: Each player tees off, and for their second shot, each player hits where their partners drive went. After both players play that second shot, the team selects the better ball as their third shot and picks up the worse ball. A scramble is played between the two partners from the third shot until the team finishes the hole.
  • Closest to the pin holes: 2, 6, 13
  • Longest drive holes: 1, 15
  • Tournament buy in: $25
  • Cost of a 'biff': $20 & a shotgun on the first tee
  • 2025 Draft Choice: Joes
